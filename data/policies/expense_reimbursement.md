# Expense Reimbursement Policy
**Sunshine Consulting**
*Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Last Revised: March 30, 2026*
*Owner: Finance Team*

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy outlines the rules and procedures for reimbursing business-related expenses incurred by employees of Sunshine Consulting. It applies to all full-time and part-time employees. Contractors must refer to their individual agreements for expense terms.

Expenses must be **business-necessary**, **reasonable**, and **pre-approved** where required. Personal expenses will not be reimbursed under any circumstances.

---

## 2. Expense Categories and Limits

### 2.1 Meals and Dining

| Location | Daily Limit (per person) |
|---|---|
| Domestic (USA) | $75 per day |
| International | $100 per day |

- Limits apply to the total of all meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner) within a single calendar day.
- Team meals (e.g., client dinners) must be pre-approved by a manager and the Finance team if the total exceeds **$200**.
- Alcohol is **not reimbursable** unless it is part of a pre-approved client entertainment event.

### 2.2 Accommodation

| Location | Nightly Limit |
|---|---|
| Domestic (USA) | $200 per night |
| International | $250 per night |

- Employees are expected to book through the preferred vendor **Egencia** whenever possible.
- Exceptions above the nightly limit require written pre-approval from the department head.

### 2.3 Ground Transportation

| Type | Policy |
|---|---|
| Rideshare (Uber/Lyft) | Reimbursable for business travel |
| Personal vehicle | $0.67 per mile (IRS standard rate) |
| Taxi | Reimbursable with receipt |
| Rental car | Economy class only; pre-approval required for upgrades |
| Public transit | Fully reimbursable |

- Personal vehicle mileage must be logged in Rippling with origin, destination, and business purpose.

### 2.4 Air Travel

- All flights must be booked through **Egencia** or with prior Finance approval.
- **Economy class** is required for flights under 6 hours.
- **Business class** may be approved for flights over 6 hours with manager and Finance sign-off.
- Seat upgrades and baggage fees beyond one checked bag are not reimbursable.

### 2.5 Other Business Expenses

| Category | Limit | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Client gifts | $50 per recipient per year | Must be pre-approved |
| Office supplies | $100 per month | No pre-approval needed |
| Professional development (books, courses) | $500 per year | Manager approval required |
| Software / subscriptions | Any amount | Must be approved by IT and Finance |

---

## 3. Receipt Requirements

- Receipts are **required for all expenses of $25 or more**.
- Receipts must show: vendor name, date, itemized list, and total amount paid.
- Credit card statements alone are **not** acceptable as receipts.
- For expenses under $25 without a receipt, a written explanation must be submitted in Rippling.

---

## 4. Submission Deadline

- All expense reports must be submitted within **30 calendar days** of the expense date.
- Expenses submitted after 30 days will not be reimbursed unless an exception is approved by the Finance team.
- Expenses from the previous calendar year must be submitted by **January 15** of the new year.

---

## 5. Submission Process

1. Log in to **Rippling** at [app.rippling.com](https://app.rippling.com)
2. Navigate to **Expenses → New Expense Report**
3. Add each expense line, upload receipt photos or scans
4. Select the appropriate expense category and project code
5. Submit to your direct manager for approval
6. Once manager-approved, Finance reviews and processes reimbursement
7. Reimbursement is issued within **5–7 business days** via direct deposit to your payroll account

---

## 6. Pre-Approval Requirements

The following expenses require written pre-approval **before** they are incurred:

- Any single expense exceeding **$500**
- International travel
- Business class flights
- Client entertainment events
- Software subscriptions or tools not on the approved IT list

Pre-approval requests must be submitted via **Rippling** under "Expense Pre-Approval" or via email to **finance@sunshineconsulting.com**.

---

## 7. Prohibited Expenses

The following are **not reimbursable** under any circumstances:

- Alcohol (unless part of a pre-approved client event)
- Personal clothing or grooming
- Gym memberships or personal fitness
- Traffic fines, parking tickets, or tolls for personal travel
- Spouse or family member travel costs
- Personal entertainment (movies, sports events, etc.)
- Expenses related to non-business activities during a business trip
- Tips exceeding 20% of the bill

---

## 8. Policy Violations

Submitting false, inflated, or duplicate expense claims constitutes fraud and may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination, as well as legal consequences. All expense reports are subject to audit by the Finance team.
