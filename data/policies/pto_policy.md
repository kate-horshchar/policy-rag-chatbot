# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy
**Sunshine Consulting**
*Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Last Revised: March 30, 2026*
*Owner: People Operations Team*

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy outlines the Paid Time Off (PTO) entitlements, accrual rules, and procedures for all full-time employees of Sunshine Consulting. Part-time employees are eligible for PTO on a pro-rated basis. Contractors and freelancers are not covered by this policy.

---

## 2. Types of Leave

Sunshine Consulting provides the following types of paid leave:

| Leave Type | Days per Year | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Annual Leave | 18 days | Accrued monthly |
| Sick Leave | 10 days | Non-accruing, available January 1 each year |
| Floating Holiday | 1 day | Can be used any time during the calendar year |
| Unpaid Leave | Upon approval | See Section 7 |

---

## 3. PTO Accrual

- Full-time employees accrue **1.5 days (12 hours) of annual leave per calendar month**.
- Accrual begins on the **first day of employment**.
- Accrued PTO is visible in real time in **Rippling** under the "Time Off" tab.
- Employees on unpaid leave do not accrue PTO during the period of absence.

### Accrual Schedule

| Months Employed | Total Accrued Days |
|---|---|
| 1 month | 1.5 days |
| 3 months | 4.5 days |
| 6 months | 9 days |
| 12 months | 18 days |

---

## 4. PTO Carryover

- Employees may carry over a maximum of **10 unused annual leave days** into the following calendar year.
- Any unused days exceeding the 10-day cap are **forfeited** on January 1.
- Sick leave does **not** carry over. Unused sick days expire at the end of each calendar year.
- People Operations will send a reminder in **November** each year to encourage employees to use any excess PTO before year-end.

---

## 5. Blackout Dates

PTO requests may not be approved during the following high-demand periods unless exceptional circumstances apply:

- **Last two weeks of each fiscal quarter** (March 16–31, June 16–30, September 16–30, December 16–31)
- **Company-wide project deadlines** as communicated by department heads at least 4 weeks in advance

During blackout periods, all PTO requests require written approval from both the direct manager and the department head. Requests submitted during blackout periods are not guaranteed approval.

---

## 6. Requesting PTO

### Advance Notice Requirements

| Duration of Leave | Notice Required |
|---|---|
| 1–2 days | At least 3 business days in advance |
| 3–5 days | At least 5 business days in advance |
| More than 5 days | At least 10 business days in advance |

### Submission Process

1. Log in to **Rippling** at [app.rippling.com](https://app.rippling.com)
2. Navigate to **Time Off → Request Time Off**
3. Select leave type, dates, and add an optional note
4. Submit — your direct manager will receive an automated approval request
5. You will be notified via email once the request is approved or declined

Verbal or Slack-only requests are **not considered official** until submitted through Rippling.

---

## 7. Unpaid Leave

Employees who have exhausted their PTO balance may request unpaid leave. Unpaid leave requires:

- Written request submitted to People Operations at least **10 business days** in advance
- Approval from the direct manager and People Operations
- Leave duration may not exceed **30 consecutive calendar days** without executive approval

---

## 8. PTO Payout Upon Termination

- Upon voluntary resignation or termination, employees will be paid out for **all accrued but unused annual leave days** in their final paycheck.
- Sick leave days are **not paid out** upon termination.
- If an employee has taken more PTO than accrued at the time of termination, the difference will be deducted from the final paycheck.

---

## 9. Sick Leave Usage

- Sick leave may be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or to care for an immediate family member.
- For absences longer than **3 consecutive sick days**, a doctor's note must be submitted to People Operations via Rippling.
- Sick leave may not be used as additional vacation days.
