# Remote Work Policy
**Sunshine Consulting**
*Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Last Revised: March 30, 2026*
*Owner: People Operations Team*

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy defines the guidelines and expectations for employees of Sunshine Consulting who work remotely on a full-time or hybrid basis. It applies to all full-time employees who have completed their probationary period. Contractors and part-time employees may work remotely subject to the terms of their individual agreements.

Remote work is a privilege, not a right, and may be modified or revoked based on business needs or performance concerns.

---

## 2. Eligibility

To be eligible for remote work, an employee must:

- Have completed the **90-day probationary period**
- Have a current performance rating of **3 or above** (on a 1–5 scale)
- Have manager approval
- Have a suitable home office environment (see Section 5)
- Have no active Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)

New employees are required to work **on-site for the first 90 days** to complete onboarding and build team relationships.

---

## 3. Remote Work Arrangements

Sunshine Consulting offers two types of remote work arrangements:

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Hybrid** | At least 2 days per week in the office; remaining days remote |
| **Fully Remote** | All work performed remotely; quarterly in-person visits required |

All arrangements must be formally approved and documented in **Rippling** before taking effect.

---

## 4. Core Hours

Regardless of time zone or work arrangement, all employees must be available during **core hours**:

- **10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone**
- During core hours, employees must be reachable via **Slack** and **email** within 30 minutes
- Meetings may be scheduled during core hours without prior consent
- Outside of core hours, employees may manage their own schedule provided work is completed

---

## 5. Home Office Requirements

Employees working remotely must maintain a workspace that meets the following standards:

### 5.1 Internet Connection
- Minimum **25 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload** speed
- Stable wired or Wi-Fi connection
- Public Wi-Fi (cafes, airports) is **not permitted** for work involving confidential client data

### 5.2 Physical Workspace
- Dedicated, quiet workspace free from excessive distractions during meetings
- Adequate lighting for video calls
- Ergonomic setup recommended (chair, desk, monitor)

### 5.3 Equipment
- Company-issued laptop must be used for all work tasks
- Personal devices may only be used for two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Employees are responsible for the safekeeping of company equipment

---

## 6. Home Office Stipend

Sunshine Consulting provides a **one-time home office stipend of $500** to eligible remote employees to cover setup costs such as a monitor, keyboard, mouse, desk accessories, or ergonomic equipment.

- Stipend is available after the **90-day probationary period**
- Must be requested through **Rippling** under "Benefits → Home Office Stipend"
- Receipts must be submitted within **60 days** of purchase
- Eligible items: monitors, keyboards, mice, webcams, headsets, desk accessories, ergonomic equipment
- Ineligible items: personal furniture, smartphones, tablets, or items unrelated to work

The stipend is a one-time benefit and does not reset annually.

---

## 7. Required In-Person Attendance

All remote employees are required to attend the office or a designated meeting location in the following cases:

- **Quarterly team meetings** — once per quarter (dates communicated at least 4 weeks in advance)
- **Client on-site visits** — as required by project needs
- **Company all-hands events** — typically held twice per year
- **Onboarding week** — mandatory for all new employees regardless of remote status

Travel and accommodation costs for required in-person events are covered by Sunshine Consulting per the Expense Reimbursement Policy.

---

## 8. Information Security Requirements

Remote employees must comply with the following security requirements at all times:

- Use the company **VPN** when accessing internal systems or confidential data
- Lock the computer screen when stepping away from the workspace
- Never share login credentials or leave devices unattended in public spaces
- Report any security incidents or device loss to the **IT team** within **2 hours** via Slack **#it-security** or email **security@sunshineconsulting.com**
- Full requirements are outlined in the **Information Security Policy**

---

## 9. Performance and Availability

Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as on-site employees. Managers may review remote work arrangements if:

- Deadlines are consistently missed
- The employee is frequently unavailable during core hours
- Performance rating drops below 3
- Client or team complaints are received

In such cases, the employee may be required to return to hybrid or full on-site work.

---

## 10. Requesting Remote Work

1. Discuss the arrangement with your direct manager
2. Log in to **Rippling** at [app.rippling.com](https://app.rippling.com)
3. Navigate to **HR Requests → Remote Work Agreement**
4. Complete the form and submit for manager and People Operations approval
5. Once approved, the arrangement is documented and takes effect on the agreed start date
