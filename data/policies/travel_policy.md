# Business Travel Policy
**Sunshine Consulting**
*Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Last Revised: March 30, 2026*
*Owner: Finance Team*

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy covers guidelines and expectations for business travel at Sunshine Consulting. It applies to all employees traveling on behalf of the company, including client visits, conferences, and internal team events.

All travel expenses must also comply with the **Expense Reimbursement Policy**.

---

## 2. Booking Travel

### Advance Booking
- All travel must be booked at least **14 days in advance** whenever possible
- Last-minute bookings (less than 14 days) require manager approval before booking

### How to Book
- Use the company's **preferred travel booking platform** (link available in Google Drive → Company Resources)
- If the platform cannot fulfill your booking, contact Finance at finance@sunshineconsulting.com for approval to book elsewhere
- Personal loyalty points (airline miles, hotel points) may be used, but the company will reimburse only up to the standard rate

---

## 3. Air Travel

| Flight Duration | Class Permitted |
|---|---|
| Under 6 hours | Economy only |
| Over 6 hours | Business class (with manager and Finance approval) |

- Book the most cost-effective option available at the time of booking
- Seat upgrades, priority boarding, and lounge access are **not reimbursable** unless included in the base fare
- One checked bag per trip is reimbursable; additional bags are at the employee's expense
- Flight cancellation or change fees are reimbursable if the change was due to business reasons

---

## 4. Accommodation

| Location | Nightly Limit |
|---|---|
| Domestic (USA) | $200 per night |
| International | $250 per night |

- Book hotels close to the primary meeting location when possible
- Exceptions above the nightly limit require written pre-approval from the department head
- Personal stays extended beyond the business trip are at the employee's own expense

---

## 5. Ground Transportation

| Type | Policy |
|---|---|
| Rideshare (Uber/Lyft) | Reimbursable for business travel |
| Taxi | Reimbursable with receipt |
| Rental car | Economy class only; pre-approval required |
| Personal vehicle | $0.67 per mile (IRS standard rate) |
| Public transit | Fully reimbursable |

- Rental cars should only be booked when other options are impractical
- Parking fees at airports or hotels are reimbursable with receipts
- Traffic fines and parking tickets are **not reimbursable**

---

## 6. Meals and Daily Allowance

Meal allowances during business travel follow the **Expense Reimbursement Policy**:

- Domestic travel: **$75 per day**
- International travel: **$100 per day**

Meals provided by a client or at a conference should not be claimed on top of the daily allowance.

---

## 7. Travel Insurance

Sunshine Consulting provides **business travel insurance** for all employees on approved company travel. Coverage includes:

- Trip cancellation due to business or medical reasons
- Emergency medical expenses abroad
- Lost or delayed baggage (up to $500)
- Travel delay compensation

Coverage is automatic for all pre-approved business trips. No separate enrollment is required. Contact Finance for a copy of the policy document.

---

## 8. International Travel

Additional requirements apply for international travel:

- Manager and Finance approval required **before booking**
- Ensure your passport is valid for at least **6 months beyond** your travel dates
- Check visa requirements in advance — visa fees are reimbursable
- Notify IT Security before traveling to ensure VPN and device access is configured for your destination
- Use the company VPN at all times when working from international locations

---

## 9. Submitting Travel Expenses

All travel expenses must be submitted within **30 days** of returning from the trip via **Rippling → Expenses → New Expense Report**. Group all expenses from a single trip into one report where possible.

Refer to the **Expense Reimbursement Policy** for full details on receipts, approval workflows, and reimbursement timelines.
