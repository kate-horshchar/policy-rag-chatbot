# Employee Onboarding Guide
**Sunshine Consulting**
*Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Last Revised: March 30, 2026*
*Owner: People Operations Team*

---

## 1. Purpose

This guide is designed to help new employees get set up and feel at home at Sunshine Consulting as quickly as possible. Your manager and the People Operations team are here to support you every step of the way.

---

## 2. Before Your First Day

People Operations will send you a welcome email with:
- Your company email address and temporary password
- A link to complete your Rippling profile and tax forms
- Your start date, time, and office location (or remote setup instructions)

Please complete the Rippling setup **before your first day** if possible.

---

## 3. Day 1: Getting Set Up

### IT Setup
- [ ] Receive and unbox your company laptop
- [ ] Log in with your company Google Workspace account
- [ ] Set up **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** on your Google account
- [ ] Install and connect to the **Cisco AnyConnect VPN**
- [ ] Set up **1Password** (company password manager) — IT will send an invite
- [ ] Join the company **Slack** workspace and set up your profile
- [ ] Access **Rippling** and complete any outstanding onboarding tasks
- [ ] Join required Slack channels: #general, #announcements, #your-team, #it-help, #hr-help

### First Meetings
- [ ] Meet with your direct manager (welcome conversation, role overview)
- [ ] Meet with your People Operations contact
- [ ] Attend the IT onboarding session (scheduled by IT team)

---

## 4. First Week

- [ ] Complete the **security awareness onboarding training** in the IT portal (required within 5 days)
- [ ] Review key company policies: PTO, Expense Reimbursement, Remote Work, Code of Conduct
- [ ] Schedule 1:1 meetings with key teammates
- [ ] Get access to relevant project tools (Notion, Jira, Google Drive — requested via #it-help)
- [ ] Attend your first team meeting
- [ ] Set up your first **30-day goals** with your manager

---

## 5. 30 / 60 / 90 Day Milestones

| Milestone | Focus |
|---|---|
| **30 days** | Learn the team, tools, and processes; complete onboarding training; establish working relationships |
| **60 days** | Start contributing independently; take ownership of first tasks or projects; give and receive feedback |
| **90 days** | Operating confidently in the role; probationary period ends; full performance expectations apply |

Your manager will check in with you at each milestone to discuss progress and answer questions.

---

## 6. Probationary Period

All new employees at Sunshine Consulting complete a **90-day probationary period** starting from their first day of employment.

During this period:
- Regular check-ins with your manager are expected
- Remote work is not available (see Remote Work Policy)
- PTO accrues normally but the home office stipend is not yet available
- Either party may end employment with shorter notice than the standard policy

At the end of 90 days, your manager will confirm successful completion in Rippling.

---

## 7. Benefits Enrollment

You have a **30-day window** from your start date to enroll in company benefits, including:

- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) with company match (up to 4% of salary)
- Life insurance
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

To enroll, go to **Rippling → Benefits → Enroll Now**. If you miss the 30-day window, you will need to wait until the next open enrollment period in **November**.
