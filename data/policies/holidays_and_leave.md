# Holidays and Leave Policy
**Sunshine Consulting**
*Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Last Revised: March 30, 2026*
*Owner: People Operations Team*

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy outlines the paid holidays and additional leave entitlements available to all full-time employees of Sunshine Consulting. These days are separate from your PTO balance and do not affect your accrued annual leave.

---

## 2. Federal Holidays

Sunshine Consulting observes the following **11 federal holidays** each year. Employees are not required to work on these days and will receive full pay.

| Holiday | Date |
|---|---|
| New Year's Day | January 1 |
| Martin Luther King Jr. Day | Third Monday in January |
| Presidents' Day | Third Monday in February |
| Memorial Day | Last Monday in May |
| Juneteenth | June 19 |
| Independence Day | July 4 |
| Labor Day | First Monday in September |
| Columbus Day | Second Monday in October |
| Veterans Day | November 11 |
| Thanksgiving Day | Fourth Thursday in November |
| Christmas Day | December 25 |

If a holiday falls on a Saturday, it is observed on the preceding Friday. If it falls on a Sunday, it is observed on the following Monday.

---

## 3. Floating Holiday

Each employee receives **1 floating holiday per calendar year** to use at any time during the year.

- The floating holiday does not carry over to the next year
- Must be requested through **Rippling** at least **2 business days** in advance
- Can be used for personal celebrations, religious observances, or any reason

---

## 4. Bereavement Leave

Sunshine Consulting provides paid bereavement leave to employees who experience the loss of a loved one.

| Relationship | Paid Leave |
|---|---|
| Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling) | 3 business days |
| Extended family (grandparent, grandchild, in-law) | 1 business day |

- Additional unpaid leave may be granted upon request to People Operations
- Employees are not required to provide documentation unless absence extends beyond the above allowances

---

## 5. Jury Duty

Employees summoned for jury duty will receive **full pay** for the duration of their service, up to **10 business days**. For jury service beyond 10 days, the employee will receive the difference between their jury stipend and their regular salary.

- Notify your manager and People Operations as soon as you receive a jury summons
- Submit a copy of the summons in **Rippling** under "HR Requests → Jury Duty"
- You are expected to return to work on days when court is not in session

---

## 6. How to Request Leave

For floating holidays, bereavement, or jury duty:

1. Log in to **Rippling** at [app.rippling.com](https://app.rippling.com)
2. Navigate to **Time Off → Request Time Off**
3. Select the appropriate leave type
4. Submit for manager approval

Federal holidays are automatically added to your calendar and do not require a request.
